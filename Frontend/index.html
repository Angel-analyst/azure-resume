About Me

Hello, I am a Mechanical Engineer turned Data Analyst and DevOps in Azure. I have a strong background in mechanical engineering, which has allowed me to develop an analytical mindset and problem-solving skills for complex challenges. Over time, my interest in technology and automation led me to explore the world of data analysis and cloud solution implementation. Currently, I specialize in data management, process optimization, and cloud infrastructure deployment using Azure. I am passionate about transforming data into valuable insights for strategic decision-making and enhancing operational efficiency through automation. My goal is to continue learning and evolving in the technology field, providing innovative solutions that create a positive impact on businesses and society.
